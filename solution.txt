Clone your fork - 'git clone https://github.com/s17314/epg1_git.git'

Create a branch named hw- (should point to master) - 'git checkout -b hw-s17314'

Rebase branch named rebase-me on top of your branch - 'git checkout rebase-me', 
'git rebase hw-s17314'

Fast forward your branch to the tip of rebase-me - 'git checkout hw-s17314', 'git 
merge f354e3d'

Merge branch named merge-me into your branch - 'git merge merge-me', 'git nano 
README.md' (rozwiązanie konfliktów), 'git add README.md', 'git commit -m ....'
